About MisoAutos

At MisoAutos, we redefine convenience, luxury, and reliability in the Nigerian automobile industry. We are a premier logistics and automotive service company, specializing in car sales, rentals, and hires for individuals and corporate clients across Nigeria. Our fleet covers a wide range of vehicles to suit every need and occasion from SUVs, sports cars, and luxury sedans to Hiace buses, Coaster buses, and heavy duty trucks. Whether you’re looking for a stylish ride for a special event, a dependable vehicle for intercity travel, or a spacious bus for group transportation, MisoAutos has you covered. We also offer professional chauffeurs and Mopol security escorts on request, ensuring your journeys are safe, comfortable, and stress-free. Our services extend to intra-state and inter-state travels, giving you the freedom to move seamlessly anywhere in Nigeria. With flexible booking options through our website and social media platforms, we make it easy for you to schedule your ride at your convenience. We also welcome affiliate partnerships, allowing others to connect with our growing network and benefit from our services. At MisoAutos, we don’t just provide cars we deliver trust, class, and peace of mind on every journey. All car models available. Your ride, your way.
